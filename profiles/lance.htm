Background

Mr. Lance Uggla serves as the President and Chief Integratio Officer of IHS Markit Ltd. Mr. Uggla has been the President of Markit Ltd. since July 12, 2016. Mr. Uggla founded Markit in 2003 and served as its Chief Executive Officer from 2003 to July 12, 2016. He was responsible for leading Markitâ€™s strategic development and managing its day-to-day operations. He has extensive experience in the management of Global Debt Capital Markets teams and Founder of Markit Group. He served as the Head of Europe and Asia at Toronto-Dominion Securities and responsible for building all offshore debt capital markets activities including its Investment Grade credit trading portfolios, which total more than USD 15 billion. Prior to joining TD Securities in 1995, he served as Head of Global Fixed Income at CIBC World Markets, where he spent nine years. Mr. Uggla served as the Chairman of Markit Ltd. until July 12, 2016.
