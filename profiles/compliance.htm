Compliance Technologies International, LLP provides global tax withholding and information return reporting technology solutions. Its products include Vendor Payment Compliance Guide, a Web-based tool used for training and implementing the withholding and reporting rules for payments made to non-U.S. vendors; Mandatory Document Due Diligence, a form validation software that allows U.S. withholding agents and qualified intermediaries to comply with the basic rules of U.S. source income; and Global Processing and Re-Solicitation Software, which is integrated within the USWA or QI’s systems to validate Forms W-8 and W-9. The company also offers Digital W8 and E-W8 that electronically automates the solicitation, renewal, and validation of Forms W-8 and W-9; Tax Withholding Software, which calculates the amount of U.S. withholding tax required on FDAP income paid to non-resident aliens; Tax reporting Software that enables USWAs and QIs to generate the IRS information return reports; and Intermediary Processing Software, which allows the U.S. withholding agents to comply with the basic rules of U.S. source income. In addition, it provides outsourcing services for document due diligence reviews, withholding, year-end tax reporting, and intermediary processing; and consulting services to help clients comply with tax withholding and information reporting rules for various types of fixed and determinable payments, financial products, and investment vehicles. It supports banks, global custodians, private equity firms, mutual funds, and hedge funds managers. It serves various clients, software and technology firms; multinational corporations in manufacturing, aerospace, entertainment, and multi-media industries, telecommunications; and consumer goods. Compliance Technologies International, LLP was founded in 1998 and is based in Andover, Massachusetts. As of July 1, 2014, Compliance Technologies International, LLP operates as a subsidiary of Markit Ltd.
