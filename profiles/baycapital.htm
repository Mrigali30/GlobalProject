    Bay Capital Partners, LLC is a boutique investment bank that provides financial and strategic advisory services. The firm offers mergers and acquisitions, corporate restructuring, private placements, strategic alliances, joint ventures and partnerships, divestitures, spin-offs, and capital transaction services. It caters to financial institutions, technology, media and telecommunications, retail, consumer products, e-commerce, and enterprise and consumer software industries. Additionally, the firm provides business valuation, strategic consulting, and capital raising services to middle market institutional and corporate clients. Bay Capital Partners, LLC is headquartered in New York, New York.
